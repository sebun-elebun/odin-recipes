Description:

A delicious pizza can always make your bad day feel a little better and one simply cannot go wrong with a fresh margherita pizza.

When done just right, margherita pizza is a feast for your taste buds, featuring fresh crusty dough, aromatic tomato sauce, melted mozarella cheese and basil leaves on top.

This pizza is very simple, fresh and yet delightful every time.

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozarella cheese
  • Basil leaves

Steps:

  1. Make fresh tomato sauce and spread it on top of the previously prepared pizza dough
  2. Sprinkle with grated mozzarella cheese
  3. Put it into the preheated to 400Â°C oven for just over 2 minutes
